About Us

"Experiments promote discovery and learning. Discovering new ideas is an integral part of learning. It is something that teachers can't give to students. Students themselves have to discover new ideas and concepts during their search for knowledge."

Hey there, I'm Swapnil, a nielitian and a programming enthusiast who loves coding and making creative things related to programming.

My interest in programming started back in 2017 when I first came to know about the 'C' programming language. After that I learnt other languages such as C++, Java, Python etc.

Back in November 2020, I created a youtube channel named Swapnil's Lab. This channel is all about programming experiments. We'll use many programming languages in our experiments and together we will learn from those experiments.

In Swapnil's Lab we make tutorials and sample projects to help people who wants to learn something new or to improve their programming skills. For further information you can write us on lab.swapnil@gmail.com or contact through this contact form.
